FIFA 22 is a soccer simulation game developed by Electronic Arts as the 29th installment in the FIFA game series.
It was released on October 1, 2021
In FIFA 22 Ultimate Team , EA has updated some game mode features
Among other things, EA has introduced FUT Heroes cards
These are cards with a unique league-specific chemistry tied to their specific hero moment, offering each player within the same league a green club link as well as the usual nation link
Players included in Team of the Week will have their player card stats boosted based on their real-world achievements to reflect the game's closeness to real-world football
There were 22 ones-to-watch players available in previous games, with an additional six added in FIFA 22
As in FIFA 21 , FIFA icons are in the game with former stars:
Iker Casillas,
Robin van Persie ,
Wayne Rooney and Cafu are newcomers
Players also have the ability to preview Silver and Gold Player Packs at certain time intervals, allowing players to see what they would receive if they made a purchase
